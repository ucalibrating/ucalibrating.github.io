• 28th april, 2021

    The Naming of Neosekai Translations.

    The name Neosekai Translations is decided upon, winning the vote over alternative proposal Conglomerate Translations.

  • 29th april, 2021

    Website is up & running!

    Yan buys the domain. Pascal, Hamlet, and Charnomi set up the website. It is cluttered with bugs and formatting issues, but those will eventually be mostly fixed.

  • 29th april, 2021

    Transfer of the first two novels.

    The first two novels, Convention and Secretly Sweet Fiancée, are posted on the website. The rest are soon to come.

  • 29th april, 2021

    Unveiling Ucali.

    Ucali migrates from Calibrating Translations, where he translated the novel OsaImo, to merge with Neosekai.

  • 1st may, 2021

    Novel pickup.

    Pascal's Wager picks up the novel Mayoi Neko Overrun. Due to the difficulty of translating "old" Japanese, it was eventually given up upon.

  • 2nd may, 2021

    Novel pickup.

    Unu picks up the novel NemuriHime, a series soon forgotten due to its lack of releases. And in collaboration with Mowaso, Unu also picks up Tottemo Kawaii Watashi to Tsukiatte yo! (the special edition, unintentionally).

  • 3rd may, 2021

    Novel pickup.

    dead picks up the novel reckless girl. Its release rate is a record-slow, charted to be growing exponentially slower with each release.

  • 6th may, 2021

    Novel pickup.

    Hamlet picks up Tonari no Kimi, proclaiming to be "a little bored of KoiNante." This novel is then TL'd by the foursome (Chaos, Charnomi, Dead, and Hamlet) and edited by Ucal until it is ultimately dropped.

  • 7th may, 2021

    News, but probably Propaganda.

    Akash gives us our first flash news brief in the new channel. Very helpful, Propaganda Eye 007.

  • 8th may, 2021

    Manga & novel pickup.

    Pascal's Wager and the rest of the manga team (Mowaso and Gathan) pick up Saint Cecilia and Pastor Lawrence. Our beloved director, Yan, picks up his first (and only) novel, Clow Card Cardcaptor.

  • 10th may, 2021

    Unu, 17.

    happy 17th birthday, Unu, and thank you. oh, and also a novel pickup by him: Consultation Girl.

  • 10th may, 2021

    Samheart Shenanigans.

    And though this was the day that Samheart joined the NeoSekai staff, it is also the day where all hell went downhill. He epithetized Dead with "dead-sama" and he is deemed a masochist by Mel due to the perverse fact that he actually completed the editing test application without losing sanity.

  • 11th may, 2021

    Lion's Launch.

    On this date, Sanguis (Leo-chan), did his first instance of assisting with translations—something he would continue to do indefinitely. You're not the lion we deserved, but the lion we needed. Thank you.

  • 13th may, 2021

    SoulWorker: NeoKami edition.

    Soulworker, a Korean MMORPG, is released globally. NeoSekai Kamis, Charnomi, Unu, and Anima (+Ucali) begin grinding. Others join but quit soon after.
